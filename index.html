About


The Plough is a 19th Century pub in the village of Potten End, we are located opposite Spencer Holland park.

The Plough caters for everybody, you can choose either the relaxed ‘snug’ bar with its open fire, the more lively public bar or even our garden.

We serve food seven days a week, please check out our menu and opening times for more information.

We host a variety of entertainment from live music, free jukebox and karaoke, please visit our events page for up to date information.

Denise, Trevor, Michaela and the team look forward to welcoming you to the pub.
